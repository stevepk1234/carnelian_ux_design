Our Legacy

Three Decades of Evolution

Active since 1991, our firm has continuously evolved — from a learning startup to Pakistan's full-service communication consulting partner with teams across four cities.

1991 — KZR Associates

Founded by Kamran Rizvi upon his return from the UK as an innovative Learning & Development firm.

2004 — Navitus

Expanded from training into full organizational development, rebranding as Navitus.

2014 — Carnelian

Added branding, coaching, and research capabilities. Became Carnelian — a full-service partner with four hubs across Pakistan.
